#### Brandon Sanderson

I'm now seven percent through writing the sequel to
*The Way of Kings*
, and I've finished the first sequence. You can track my progress in the sidebar on my website, and I often talk about it on
[Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/Mistborn)
and
[Twitter](https://twitter.com/BrandSanderson)
. My goal is to finish the first draft by April 2013, and if I can pull that off, I'll try to get the book released by Christmas 2013. I should know better in April whether that is possible.
