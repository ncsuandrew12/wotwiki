# [Interview #75: ACOS Signing Report - Erica Sadun, Entry #13](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=75#13)

#### Question

Is "Ogier" from "to sing Ogier"?

#### Robert Jordan

No. It's to sound like Ogre but not be exactly the same. Name of family in Charleston.

#### Footnote

It's also
[a small street](http://maps.google.com/maps?q=charleston+ogier+st+map)
not far from where RJ lived with Harriet in the historic district of Charleston.
