# [Interview #1155, Entry #46]: Calamity-Philadelphia, PA](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1155#46)

#### Question

How do you build names?

#### Brandon Sanderson

Third of the time, makes up linguistics, third of the time steals a convention from something in Earth, a third of the time is like Reckoners where he just comes up with it.
