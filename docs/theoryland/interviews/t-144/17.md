# [Interview #144: Elf Fantasy Fair - Aan'allein, Entry #17](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=144#17)

#### Question

Is fantasy also disregarded in the USA?

#### Robert Jordan

Yes. There is an
[exception](http://www.nytimes.com/1996/12/08/books/flaming-swords-and-wizards-orbs.html)
in [Edward Rothstein]...he works for the
*New York Times*
, a culture editor, not a literary editor. But that's very good. He seems to understand what I'm writing about. But fantasy is by and large dismissed as less.
