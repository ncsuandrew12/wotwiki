#### Outis

Are you going to make any of your primary characters into worldhoppers?

#### Brandon Sanderson

Some of them already are. There are primary characters who are worldhoppers. We just haven't had books about them yet.
In this series' main characters, I will RAFO whether any will become worldhoppers.
