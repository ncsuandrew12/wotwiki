# [Interview #1076: WoR Signing Report - Bartbug, Entry #4](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1076#4)

#### Question

I was interested in the implications of Gaz coming back.

#### Brandon Sanderson

Gaz was always planned to come back. Which is why there's kind of a mystery to him in the first book, like where did he go? It's something Robert Jordan did that I liked.
