# [Interview #36: Twitter 2011 (WoT), Entry #175](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=36#175)

#### [Dennis (14 November 2011)](http://twitter.com/accelerate/status/136145222697627648)

Has writing WoT affected the way you write your other books?

#### [Brandon Sanderson (14 November 2011)](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/136147839821684736)

Some of them. I look at detail differently, foreshadowing differently, and large casts differently now.
