# [Interview #36: Twitter 2011 (WoT), Entry #176](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=36#176)

#### [Eric Pearson (14 November 2011)](http://twitter.com/mynameisdeuce/status/136145704509911040)

Since the series is almost complete, if RJ could read it how would he react to your contribution to his legacy?

#### [Brandon Sanderson (14 November 2011)](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/136149160234725376)

I honestly don't know. I hope he'd be pleased, but I also feel he'd be sad that he didn't get to do it himself.

#### [BRANDON SANDERSON](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/136149241193177088)

Harriet has said she thinks, from above, he's looking down and this is his reaction:

#### [BRANDON SANDERSON](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/136149362672807936)

First, a "Who does that kid think he is?" followed soon by a proud "That's my book, more or less."
