# [Interview #428: BarnesandNoble.com Book Club Q&A with Brandon Sanderson, Entry #52](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=428#52)

#### myserylover12

When is the next
*Alcatraz*
book coming out?

#### Brandon Sanderson

*Alcatraz Versus the Knights of Crystallia*
is in stores October 1st (though Scholastic often ships early, so you might find it as early as September 1st.) Book four—
*Alcatraz Versus the Shattered Lens*
—is being written now and will come out the following October.
