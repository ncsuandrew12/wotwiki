# [Interview #1067: Steelheart Release Party, Entry #26](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1067#26)

#### Question

Are Inquisitors bald?

#### Brandon Sanderson

Yes.

#### Question

Are they bald by being bald, or do they shave their heads?

#### Brandon Sanderson

They shave their heads. Hemalurgy does not automatically make you bald.
