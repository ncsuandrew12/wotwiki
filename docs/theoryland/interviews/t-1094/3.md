# [Interview #1094: Salt Lake City Comic-Con 2014, Entry #3](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1094#3)

#### Question

What were these Szeth things for...

#### Brandon Sanderson

Those were little plastic things you snap out and he stand up. So he's a little figure to have at your cubicle or something like that.

#### Question

I just saw you post about leaving them in places on twitter...

#### Brandon Sanderson

We will be doing things like that for other books as well so keep an eye out for that.
