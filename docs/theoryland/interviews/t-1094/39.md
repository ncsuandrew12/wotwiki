# [Interview #1094: Salt Lake City Comic-Con 2014, Entry #39](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1094#39)

#### Question

*Dragonsteel*
, where in the universe is that going to take place?

#### Brandon Sanderson

It's actually first.

#### Question

Oh really? So it's like a prequel to everything?

#### Brandon Sanderson

Yes, to the cosmere.

#### Question

So is it going to do the breaking of the Shards?

#### Brandon Sanderson

Yep.
