# [Interview #997: The Rithmatist: A Chat with Brandon Sanderson, Entry #9](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=997#9)

#### The Book Smugglers

**About the Author:**

Brandon Sanderson is the bestselling author of books including
*Warbreaker*
,
*Elantris*
,
*The Way of Kings*
, and The Mistborn Trilogy—
*Mistborn*
,
*The Well of Ascension*
and
*The Hero of Ages*
. He has also written
*Alcatraz Versus the Evil Librarians*
, a book for middle-grade readers, and completed the final books in Robert Jordan's Wheel of Time series—
*The Gathering Storm*
,
*Towers of Midnight*
and
*A Memory of Light*
—based on Jordan's notes and material. Sanderson teaches writing at Brigham Young University. He lives in Utah.

You can find out more about Brandon and
*The Rithmatist*
on his
[blog](http://www.brandonsanderson.com/blog)
, on
[twitter](https://twitter.com/BrandSanderson)
(@BrandSanderson), on
[facebook](https://www.facebook.com/BrandSanderson)
, and on his
[website](http://www.brandonsanderson.com/)
.

Also make sure to check out Brandon's upcoming event schedule for a chance to see him a city near you and get an autographed copy of
*The Rithmatist*
(people in New York, Brandon will be here tomorrow at Books of Wonder!).

And of course, make sure to stop by later today as Thea reviews
*The Rithmatist*
. Thank you, Brandon!
