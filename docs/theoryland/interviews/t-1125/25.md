# [Interview #1125: Changing Hands, AZ, Entry #25](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1125#25)

#### Leinton

Is Soulcasting blood a valid cure for blood diseases?

#### Brandon Sanderson

Not really, it's super hard to soulcast people/bodies, you’d have to get it so exact - their blood, healthy blood. It's very hard - basically you’d probably kill them in the process.
