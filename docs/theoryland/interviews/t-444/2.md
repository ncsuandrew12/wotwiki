#### Brandon Sanderson

This weekend I'm going to be the Toastmaster at
[MileHiCon](http://milehicon.org/)

[Koelbel Library on Thursday](http://brandonsanderson.com/events)

[November 1st](http://brandonsanderson.com/events)
during the tour.)
