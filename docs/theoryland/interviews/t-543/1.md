# [Interview #543: New York ComicCon Signing Report - Ted Herman, Entry #1](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=543#1)

#### Ted Herman

Can a Dreamer access
*Tel'aran'rhiod*
if trapped by an
*a'dam*
?

#### Brandon Sanderson

At first, Brandon said yes, but then he qualified that with 90% chance of yes.
