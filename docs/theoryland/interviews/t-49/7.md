# [Interview #49: LOC Signing Report - Matthew Hunter, Entry #7](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=49#7)

#### Robert Jordan

Newsflash!

The covers aren't as bad as we thought they were. The 'extra' character in
*The Eye of the World*
really was in the book, but was cut out later, because he had too little to do. His parts were distributed out to the other
characters, but they never got around to cutting him from the cover.
