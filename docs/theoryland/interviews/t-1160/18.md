# [Interview #1160: Shadows of Self-Oak Brook, IL, Entry #18](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1160#18)

#### Question

Since Vasher has worldhopped to Roshar, is he still immortal?

#### Brandon Sanderson

As long as he has Breath or Investiture to consume, then yes.

#### Question

Yes.
