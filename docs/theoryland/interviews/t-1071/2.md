# [Interview #1071, Entry #2]: WoR Signing Report - Pinpoint](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1071#2)

#### Pinpoint

If Hoid got beheaded, would his body grow a new head?

#### Brandon Sanderson

Yes.

#### Pinpoint

What if Hoid got cut by a shardblade?

#### Brandon Sanderson

The Shardblade cuts the soul and what Hoid does heals the soul.
