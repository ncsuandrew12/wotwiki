# [Interview #897: Twitter 2013 (Non-WoT), Entry #17](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=897#17)

#### [Michael M Lane (23 January 2013)](https://twitter.com/Michael_M_Lane/status/294188530945622016)

Afternoon. What sorts of marketing have you found most effective for your books, Brandon?

#### [Brandon Sanderson (23 January 2013)](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/294193318672359424)

When I was newer, it was visiting bookstores, giving free copies to their SF readers on staff.

#### [Brandon Sanderson](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/294193452432887808)

These days, I'm honestly not sure. The momentum of it all has pretty much overwhelmed my individual efforts. :)
