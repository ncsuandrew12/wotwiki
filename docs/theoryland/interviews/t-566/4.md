# [Interview #566: TOM Signing Report - Robert Mee, Entry #4](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=566#4)

#### Harriet McDougal Rigney

Harriet was asked to pronounce the Forsaken names. I had my BWB with me, which she borrowed so she didn't forget anyone. She also joked as she said Moghedien's name that Jim had said all of the Forsaken women were different sides of her, and that Moghedien was probably from when she had to remind Jim that he hadn't taken out the garbage. Even
*New York Times*
best-selling authors need to do housework apparently.
