# [Interview #988: West Jordan Signing Report - Josh & Mi'ch, Entry #20](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=988#20)

#### Question

What is the technology level of the singular society that existed when Adonalsium Shattered?

#### Brandon Sanderson

What was the technology level of the society that existed when Adonalsium Shattered? It was less than our own.

#### QUESTION

Are you going to give us anything more specific than that?

#### BRANDON SANDERSON

Less than our own.

[Yolen was the first planet with people](http://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=977#198)
