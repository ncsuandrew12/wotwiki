# [Interview #988: West Jordan Signing Report - Josh & Mi'ch, Entry #5](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=988#5)

#### Question

So, is it next year, 2013 that [Stormlight 2] will come out?

#### Brandon Sanderson

2013, it’ll be next year.

#### QUESTION

At the end of the- like the fall?

#### BRANDON SANDERSON

Yep, I am a quarter of the way through it, and as long as I turn in an early draft by April they’ll be able to do it by the fall.

#### Footnote

The release date is currently scheduled at March 4, 2014. It is still subject to change.
