# [Interview #198: DragonCon Signing Reports - Matt Hatch, Entry #16](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=198#16)

#### Matt Hatch

The Finns reside in a Parallel World, is that correct?

#### Robert Jordan

Yes.

#### Matt Hatch

No, they are different.

Possibly.
