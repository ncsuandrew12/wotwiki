# [Interview #727: Hero of Ages Q&A - TWG, Entry #40](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=727#40)

#### [darxbane (16 October 2008)](http://twg.17thshard.com/index.php?topic=6655.msg129171#msg129171)

When the Kandra get their spikes back, do they remember who they were, or have they been "rebooted"?

#### [Brandon Sanderson (17 October 2008)](http://twg.17thshard.com/index.php?topic=6655.msg129238#msg129238)

They remember, though some things are vague or foggy in their minds. Depends on the individual.
