# [Interview #727: Hero of Ages Q&A - TWG, Entry #48](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=727#48)

#### [Kaimipono (16 October 2008)](http://twg.17thshard.com/index.php?topic=6655.msg129205#msg129205)

Very minor question—was Reen a Misting or Mistborn, and if so, did he end up as a spike in an inquisitor somewhere?

#### [Brandon Sanderson (17 October 2008)](http://twg.17thshard.com/index.php?topic=6655.msg129246#msg129246)

Reen had no Allomantic powers, I'm afraid. His father was skaa. (He was Vin's half-brother, if you recall.) And yes, he is dead.
