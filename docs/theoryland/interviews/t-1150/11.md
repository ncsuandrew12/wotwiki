# [Interview #1150: Librarypalooza, Entry #11](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1150#11)

#### eagle

How close are all of the shard worlds in space?

#### Brandon Sanderson

(Not verbatum) The cosmere takes place in a dwarf galaxy and all the worlds are close together.

#### eagle

Close as in say 10 light-years?

#### Brandon Sanderson

More like 50. He went on to say that Peter has some harder numbers and that it might have to change a little.
