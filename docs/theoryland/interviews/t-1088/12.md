# [Interview #1088, Entry #12]: WoR Signing Report - Argent](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1088#12)

#### Question

Who would win in a fight, Kaladin or Kelsier.

#### Brandon Sanderson

Kelsier would probably find him in his sleep and kill him there. I would say Kelsier because he
*would not*
fight fairly.
