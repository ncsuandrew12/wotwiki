# [Interview #923: Ekultúra: Interview with Robert Jordan, Entry #16](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=923#16)

#### Galgóczi Móni

I have read stories about bringing these books to the big screen. Do you write the script?

#### Robert Jordan

No, because it's not my thing. Though they bought the option for the books several years ago and we talked about films, TV and mini-series as well, so far nothing has come of it. Otherwise, the Dragonmount.com website has now started to produce an animated movie from the first volume of the series,
*The Eye of the World*
.
