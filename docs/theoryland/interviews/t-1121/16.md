# [Interview #1121, Entry #16]: Brandon Sanderson Book Signing at JordanCon 8](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=1121#16)

#### Question

What is your favorite book?

#### Brandon Sanderson

My favorite is Dragons’ Bane by Barbara Hambly. It may not be the best, but it’s my favorite since it made me want to be a fantasy writer.
