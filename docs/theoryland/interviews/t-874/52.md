# [Interview #874: Twitter 2013 (WoT), Entry #52](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=874#52)

#### [David Forbes (23 January 2013)](https://twitter.com/DavidForbes18/status/294189871269027844)

Can you shed any light on the raven picture Min saw around Carlinya? Or the axe and hand she saw around Elayne?

#### [Brandon Sanderson (23 January 2013)](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/294202192221573120)

One thing that was made clear in the notes is that not everything seen by Min is to have significance for the books.

#### [Brandon Sanderson](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/294202412770660352)

And others, such as Alivia, were to be things that people in world placed much import upon—but were actually minor.
