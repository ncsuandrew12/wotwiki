# [Interview #874: Twitter 2013 (WoT), Entry #44](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=874#44)

#### [Austen Woods (23 January 2013)](https://twitter.com/austenw/status/294189026666225664)

Two questions: Where was Shaidar Haran during The Last Battle? Were all the major deaths planned by RJ?

#### [Brandon Sanderson (23 January 2013)](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/294197319526662145)

The husk that Rand sees, which gave birth to the void. Second: Almost all, but a few were left open and decided by us.

#### [Andy Nogar](http://twitter.com/cursor_mortis/status/294191422154895361)

What happened to Shaidar Haran? He seems to have dropped off the face of Randland.

#### [Brandon Sanderson](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/294208078260342784)

Rand sees him as a husk that gave birth to the void.
