# [Interview #874: Twitter 2013 (WoT), Entry #63](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=874#63)

#### [Lisa Mercer (23 January 2013)](https://twitter.com/Lokapele/status/294191082508517376)

When was the second time that Mat had died? Was it when he was 'reborn' with a new name from his marriage?

#### [Brandon Sanderson (23 January 2013)](http://twitter.com/BrandSanderson/status/294206911300112385)

Rahvin's attack.

#### Footnote

For the record, this is the only time Mat died; there was nothing in what the Aelfinn said about him dying twice, though presumably he'll die again some day.
