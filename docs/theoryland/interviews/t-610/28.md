# [Interview #610: London Signing Report - Thomas C, Entry #28](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=610#28)

#### Thomas C.

One fan got Sanderson to do his signature on their iPad, which led to the representative from Forbidden Planet getting a picture of this! Sanderson was extremely impressed with how his signature turned out!

#### Brandon Sanderson

Sanderson confirmed he would be appearing at
[Supanova](http://www.supanova.com.au/)
in Australia in February 2012 (but not which city) as well as
[SwanCon](https://2012.swancon.com.au/)
in Perth in April 2012.
