## Entry #1

#### [Grant (8 January 2013)](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288811259145617408)

Harriet now reading from

#### Footnote

There was supposed to be a reading and Q&A period with both Brandon and Harriet at this event. However, Brandon's plane was late, so the reading and Q&A proceeded with just Harriet.

## Entry #2

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288812895746269184)

Will any of the notes be published?

#### Harriet McDougal

No, but an encyclopedia will come out in about a year.

## Entry #3

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288813137615011841)

Movie?

#### Harriet McDougal

Universal has the rights and has had them for more than 5 years.

## Entry #4

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288813267600687104)

Hardest part of Brandon finishing the series?

#### Harriet McDougal

That he wasn't my husband.

## Entry #5

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288813611680407552)

How do you feel now that this part of your life is over?

#### Harriet McDougal

I don't know but I bought an embroidery kit.

## Entry #6

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288813913280225280)

Will the audio of RJ's dictation of the final scene be released?

#### Harriet McDougal

That's a good question. I'll think about it.

## Entry #7

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288814055853027329)

Which female character are you most like?

#### Harriet McDougal

Moghedien.

## Entry #8

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288814325991342080)

Who is your favorite character?

#### Harriet McDougal

Verin, I'm a Brown. But I'm a lot like Nynaeve.

## Entry #9

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288814951089459200)

Will you edit any more books?

#### Harriet McDougal

There are only 2 sentences of material for outrigger novels. RJ did not want infinite more books.

## Entry #10

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288815194216468480)

Any other RJ books?

#### Harriet McDougal

There was a western but it was too rough and needs a rewrite. It will not come out.

## Entry #11

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288816075221659648)

What about the main character that was removed from
*The Eye of the World*
?

#### Harriet McDougal

He didn't do anything until book 4. So he got cut.

## Entry #12

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288816442399412224)

Staircase documentary?

#### Harriet McDougal

Hunter shot RJ hamming it up for 2 hours. Coming into shape. Might come out.

#### Footnote

This question is in reference to
["The Wit of the Staircase"](http://wots.spiralent.com/wots/The_Wit_of_the_Staircase.html)
, a planned film on RJ and southern writers by Hunter Wentworth.

## Entry #13

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288816659521744896)

What trait of a female character did you do? Braid tugging?

#### Harriet McDougal

None.

## Entry #14

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288816885238210560)

Who was RJ most like?

#### Harriet McDougal

Perrin and also Mat. He quit a good job to be an author so you know he's a gambler.

## Entry #15

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288817698308247552)

What was it like editing as Team Jordan?

#### Harriet McDougal

It was a team process—military history, characters, and continuity.

## Entry #16

#### [Question ()](https://twitter.com/grantosan/status/288818013782818816)

How do you get to be an editor for an author like RJ?

#### Harriet McDougal

I started a long time ago when dinosaurs roamed the earth! Worked my way up.

#### Footnote

Harriet was already an established editor when she met RJ; it was RJ who was just starting out as an author. See
[Theoryland's interview with Harriet](http://www.theoryland.com/news-wot.php?item=167)
for more details.
