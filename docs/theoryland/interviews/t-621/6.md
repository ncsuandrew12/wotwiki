# [Interview #621: WorldCon 2011 WoT Panel - Linda Taglieri, Entry #6](https://www.theoryland.com/intvmain.php?i=621#6)

#### Linda Taglieri

Someone asked Matt Hatch and myself what we'd do after the series is finished. Matt said that not all answers would be given in the books and so discussion can, and no doubt would, continue. There are also always newcomers to the series. So Theoryland will be around. I have articles I want to write on various characters and aspects of the series. I haven't finished the read-through, either. Which reminds me: there are a few theories I want to write before
*A Memory of Light*
is out...
